Personal Background

I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Systems at the National University of General Sarmiento in Buenos Aires. My academic journey is steering me toward a deep understanding of secure, scalable, and efficient software development.

Throughout my career, I have been committed to crafting high-quality software solutions, with a keen focus on understanding client needs and ensuring user satisfaction. My dedication to meticulous attention to detail and excellence is matched by my commitment to collaborative teamwork. By working closely with diverse teams, I strive to foster an environment where collective ideas and efforts lead to superior results and enhanced project success.

Professionally, I have honed my skills across various industries, including software factories, e-commerce, and business management systems. These roles have enriched my technical abilities and provided invaluable insights into optimizing workflow processes and enhancing system functionalities.

While my primary role has been deeply technical, my experience extends beyond coding. It encompasses thorough engagement with the lifecycle of software development and a consistent focus on creating impactful and user-centric solutions.
